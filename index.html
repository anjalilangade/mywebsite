Anjali Langade is a 500-Hour Registered Yoga Teacher (RYT) certified by the Yoga Alliance, specialising in Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga. With over three years of dedicated personal practice and a deep understanding of the Primary and Intermediate Ashtanga Series, she brings precision, warmth, and clarity to every class she leads.

"Yoga is not about touching your toes. It's about what you learn on the way down."

Her journey into yoga began alongside a rigorous academic career — holding an M.Tech in Transportation Engineering from NIT Warangal — which gave her a uniquely analytical lens for understanding body mechanics, alignment, and the science behind movement. This blend of intellect and intuition makes her instruction exceptionally clear and grounded.

During her 500-Hour Teacher Training, Anjali immersed herself in Pranayama, Yoga Theory, Ayurveda, Teaching Methodology, and Asana Alignment — building a holistic toolkit that goes far beyond the physical postures. She is passionate about helping beginners build confidence, break through hesitation, and discover a practice that is truly their own.

Whether you are stepping onto the mat for the very first time or deepening an existing practice, Anjali creates a space that is welcoming, non-judgmental, and deeply transformative.
